Animal Behavior:

Sleeping Habits:

Three toed sloths are mostly nocturnal and spend 90% of their time motionless. Two toed sloths are nocturnal.

Protection from Predators:

Algae grows in its hair providing natural camouflage. The fact that they move so slowly also provides protection from predators

Food gathering:

They use smell and touch to find food
